Live economic model for OSR, Oil Rigs, and Mining Shafts.

1 · The Economic Loop

01

Burn OSR + a small ETH fee to deploy virtual nodes on your compound.

02

Mints burn 70% of the OSR cost, route 30% to treasury. Upgrades & crates split 50/30/20.

03

A halving curve pays OSR from a pre-minted 229M reserve, share-capped at 30% per user.

04

Compound upgrades unlock more slots, more crates, and higher rarity pools.

4 · Halving Emission Model

E(t) = 262 OSR/sec × 0.5 ^ (t / 7 days)
Day 0   :  262   OSR/sec   (22.6M/day)
Day 7   :  131   OSR/sec   (50% of lifetime emitted)
Day 14  :  65.5  OSR/sec   (75%)
Day 30  :  13.4  OSR/sec   (95%)
Day 90  :  ~0              (emission extinct)

Lifetime emission = 229M OSR, pre-minted to the protocol-owned reserve at genesis. Mint authority is revoked post-distribution — no new supply can ever be created.

What is OSR?

Oil Strategic Reserve is a gamified, on-chain node-mining platform on Robinhood Chain. You burn $OSR to deploy virtual Oil Rigs and Mining Shafts on your own 3D compound. Those nodes produce rewards over time, paid from a 229M $OSR emission reserve released via a Bitcoin-style halving curve.

Underground op. Compoundable, bonus slots at L5/L7/L9. Slots: Drill Bit, Ore Cart, Rail Track, Shaft Elevator.
